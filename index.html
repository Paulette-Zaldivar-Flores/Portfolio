My name is Paulette Zaldivar Flores. I am originally from San Diego, California and currently based in Tokyo, Japan. Skills that I have found to be more than profitable as a new frontend developer. I began my coding journey last year November 2021 and have been coding everyday since. I am currently open to being part of an inclusive team that fosters professional growth, empathy and creativity.
